# 🛒 Blinkit Online Shopping Sales & Performance Analysis

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing sales metrics, customer ratings, outlet characteristics, and product inventory performance for Blinkit (online grocery application).

---

## 📌 Project Overview
This project provides data-driven insights into sales distribution across different retail outlets, product categories, fat content classification, and geographic tiers. The dashboard enables stakeholders to evaluate business performance dynamically through key metrics and category breakdowns.

---

## 📊 Key Insights & Metrics

* **Total Sales:** $1.2M
* **Average Sales per Transaction:** $140.99
* **Total Items Sold:** 8.52K items
* **Average Customer Rating:** 3.92 / 5.00

### Visual Highlights:
1. **Sales by Outlet Establishment Year:** Historical sales trend analysis highlighting key peak performance years (~$205K peak around 2018).
2. **Fat Content Breakdown:** ~64.6% ($776.3K) of sales come from Low Fat items versus ~35.4% ($425.4K) from Regular items.
3. **Top Item Categories:** Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods lead sales volume, followed by Household and Frozen Foods.
4. **Outlet Size Distribution:** Medium-sized outlets drive the largest revenue share (42.27%), followed by Small (37.0%) and High (20.72%).
5. **Location Tier Analysis:** Tier 3 locations generate the highest revenue ($472.13K), followed by Tier 2 ($393.15K) and Tier 1 ($336.40K).
